A nonpartisan way to estimate how Carbon Washington's proposal impacts you

A proposal, from Carbon Washington, would impose a carbon tax and use the revenues to reduce sales taxes and business taxes, and to fund a Working Families Rebate for low-income households.

Household Calculator

Use our household calculator to discover how Carbon Washington's proposal will affect you and your household. Answer a few questions and we'll crunch the numbers for you. The calculator will take into account savings from the reduced sales tax and the Working Families Rebate and estimate what you would pay due to taxes on fossil fuels for driving and flying, and powering and heating your home.

Business Calculator

Want to see how Carbon Washington's proposal will affect your business? Answer a few questions and our calculator will estimate the amount of money your business would save from the reduced sales tax and the elimination of the B&O Manufacturing Tax as well as what you would pay due to taxes on fossil fuels for driving and flying for business, and powering and heating your business.
