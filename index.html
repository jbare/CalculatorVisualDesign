Carbon Tax Calculator

A nonpartisan way to estimate how Carbon Washington's proposal impacts you

A proposal, from Carbon Washington, would impose a carbon tax and use the revenues to reduce sales taxes and business taxes, and to fund a Working Families Rebate for low-income households.

Get a nonpartisan estimate

We don't want to sway you one way or the other.

This website is not intended to advocate for or against this policy, but to provide an unbiased estimate of the impacts of the policy to give voters more information upon which to base their voting decision.

Our calculations and methodology are transparent

Individual Calculator

Use our individual calculator to discover how Carbon Washington's proposal will affect you. Answer a few questions about XX and YY and we'll crunch the numbers for you. The calculator will show you an estimate of the amount of money you would save from the reduced sales tax and the Working Families Rebate.

The calculator will also show you an estimate of the amount you would pay from the new tax on the burning of fossil fuels, from activities such as driving vehicles, flying on airplanes, and powering and heating your home.

Business Calculator

Use our business calculator to discover how Carbon Washington's proposal will affect your business. Answer a few questions about XX and YY and we'll crunch the numbers for you. It will show you an estimate of the amount of money your business would save from the reduced sales tax and the elimination of the B&O Manufacturing Tax.

It will also show you an estimate of the amount your business would pay from the new tax on the burning of fossil fuels, from activities such as driving vehicles, flying on airplanes, and powering and heating your building.
